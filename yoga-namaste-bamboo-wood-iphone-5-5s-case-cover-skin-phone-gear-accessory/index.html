Yoga Namaste Bamboo Wood iPhone 5 5S Case Cover Skin Phone Gear Accessory

$20.00

Product Description

Protect your valuable iPhone 5 / 5S with a one-of-a-kind bamboo case. The laser engraved artwork features a stylized yoga figure in the "Namaste" pose. The gesture Namaste represents the belief that there is a Divine Spark within each of us that is located in the heart chakra. The gesture is an acknowledgment of the soul in one by the soul in another. The iPhone 5 / 5S cover design also includes words citing the benefits of yoga, such as relaxation, mindfulness, health, balance, strength, calm, flow, zen, breathe, serenity, and peace in a flowing font. These cases are the perfect yoga accessory or gift for yoga instructors. This auction is for one case only. All iMakeTheCase designs are original and not found elsewhere.

NOTE: These do not fit the new iPhone 5C.

Why Our Bamboo Cases are Different 
Our unique one piece design, and PVC trim, provides sleek aesthetics and snaps on firmly for greater durability and safety for your expensive iPhone 5 / 5S. We choose not to sell cheaper, two piece bamboo phone covers, because they are prone to splinter. 

 
Why We Love Working with Bamboo
Our iPhone 5 / 5S cases are made with elegant bamboo wood. Bamboo is revered as "eco-friendly" because it's fast-growing and can yield 20 times more wood than trees. Bamboo also releases 35% more oxygen than trees of similar size. The Japanese expression "wabi-sabi" sums up the wonder of bamboo. It means, there's beauty in imperfection. We love that each bamboo skin has it's own unique coloration, grain and flow, and we value the opportunity to be connected to nature with these small treasures. We think it's the perfect ying, to the high tech iPhone yang!
