Description

Protect your valuable iPhone 5 5S with a one-of-a-kind bamboo case. The laser engraved artwork features a zombie glossary design inspired by the Walking Dead TV series, and World War Z and Night of the Living Dead films. Our case is the perfect gift for all zombie lovers, Walking Dead television show fans, and anyone looking for a sleek ghoulish iPhone case. All iMakeTheCase designs are original and not found elsewhere.

NOTE: These do not fit the iPhone 5C.

We offer FREE SHIPPING to the United States.

Our unique one piece design, and PVC trim, provides sleek aesthetics and snaps on firmly for greater durability and safety for your expensive iPhone 5 5S. We choose not to sell cheaper, two piece bamboo phone covers, because they are prone to splinter.

Our iPhone 5 5S cases are made with elegant bamboo wood. Bamboo is revered as "eco-friendly" because it's fast-growing and can yield 20 times more wood than trees. Bamboo also releases 35% more oxygen than trees of similar size. The Japanese expression "wabi-sabi" sums up the wonder of bamboo. It means, there's beauty in imperfection. We love that each bamboo skin has it's own unique coloration, grain and flow, and we value the opportunity to be connected to nature with these small treasures. We think it's the perfect yin, to the high tech iPhone yang!
