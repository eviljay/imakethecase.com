Why We Love Working with Bamboo
Our iPhone cases are made with elegant bamboo wood. Bamboo is revered as "eco-friendly" because it's fast-growing and can yield 20 times more wood than trees. Bamboo also releases 35% more oxygen than trees of similar size. The Japanese expression "wabi-sabi" sums up the wonder of bamboo. It means, there's beauty in imperfection. We love that each bamboo skin has it's own unique coloration, grain and flow, and we value the opportunity to be connected to nature with these small treasures.
